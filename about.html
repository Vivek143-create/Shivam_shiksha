About Shivam Shiksha

Shivam Shiksha Samiti, Karauli is a non-governmental organization registered on 21 January 2006 under the Society Act 1958. The organization was established with the objective of developing education in the society and spreading public welfare schemes to benefit the deprived class. It has been 14 years since the organization has been working on various issues.
